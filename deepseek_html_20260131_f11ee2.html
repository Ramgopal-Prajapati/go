About Golden Leaf Tea House

Founded in 2010, Golden Leaf Tea House began as a small boutique in London's Covent Garden. Today, we're recognized globally for our commitment to sourcing only the finest teas from sustainable gardens around the world.

Our master blenders travel to remote regions of China, Japan, India, Sri Lanka, and Kenya to personally select leaves at the peak of their flavor. We work directly with farmers who share our values of ethical practices and environmental stewardship.

Every tea in our collection tells a story - from the misty mountains of Darjeeling to the ancient tea forests of Yunnan. We're not just selling tea; we're offering an experience, a moment of peace in a busy world.

Meet Our Founder

Eleanor Chen

With a family history in tea cultivation spanning five generations in Taiwan, Eleanor's passion for tea began in her grandmother's garden. After studying botany at Oxford, she combined scientific knowledge with traditional wisdom to create Golden Leaf.

"Tea is more than a beverage - it's a ritual, a moment of mindfulness, a connection to nature and to each other. My mission is to share this experience with the world."

"I believe that in each cup of properly brewed tea, you can taste the care of the farmer, the skill of the processor, and the history of centuries of tradition. That's why we're meticulous about every step - from leaf to cup."

Our Technology & Process

We combine centuries-old traditions with modern technology to ensure every cup meets our exacting standards of quality and flavor.

Precision Temperature Control

Computer-controlled water heating systems ensure optimal brewing temperature for each tea type.

Leaf Integrity Scanning

Advanced imaging technology assesses leaf quality, size, and color consistency before packaging.

Nitrogen Flush Packaging

Oxygen-free packaging technology preserves freshness and flavor for up to 24 months.

Laboratory Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing for purity, flavor profile, and antioxidant levels.
